# Cinema 4D Plugin

> This guide explains how to connect RenderTracker with Cinema 4D.

---

## 1. Installation

Check the **`Cinema 4D`** version in the installer and install it.  
RenderTracker will be connected automatically with no additional setup required.

---

## 2. Launch RenderTracker App

Click **`Extensions > RenderTracker`** in the Cinema 4D top menu to launch the app.  
![c4d-rendertracker | w-600](res/rendertracker/c4d-rendertracker.webp)

---

## 3. Enable Thumbnails (Optional)

To display thumbnails in RenderTracker, **`Save`** must be enabled in Cinema 4D's **`Render Settings`**.  
![c4d-save | w-600](res/rendertracker/c4d-save.webp)

> ⚠️ The current version does not support tracking AOV sub-paths from Octane or Redshift.

---

## ⚠️ Notes

*   Cinema 4D only sends text-based render information to RenderTracker, so there is virtually no impact on actual rendering performance.
